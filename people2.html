Our People

The proposed research team will involve local SMU economics faculty members who have expertise in the related areas, led by Pao-Li CHANG (as the lead PI) and Lin MA / Jing LI (as the Co-Investigators). The local research team will be joined by a panel of international experts: Daniel XU, Ayumu Ken KIKKAWA and Yuhei MIYAUCHI (each with established research profiles in production network), and Davin CHOR (with strong expertise in GVC).

Faculty Members

Lead PI: Pao-Li CHANG

is Associate Professor of Economics and Associate Dean (Research) at SMU. Her areas of research expertise include empirical and quantitative analyses of trade flows and labor market response, empirical analyses of global value chain, and analyses of firm-level micro performances. She will take charge of the data acquisition process and the communication with public/private sources for obtaining the data. She will spearhead research projects under the research themes of productivity estimation, GVC, trade/labor, and semiconductor industry. She has been PI for several research grants, including an MOE Tier 2 grant. She is full-time at SMU and will devote 35% effort to the project.
