Faculty Members from SMU

Lead PI: Pao-Li CHANG

Chang Pao-Liis Associate Professor of Economics and Associate Dean (Research) at SMU. Her areas of research expertise include empirical and quantitative analyses of trade flows and labor market response, empirical analyses of global value chain, and analyses of firm-level micro performances. She will take charge of the data acquisition process and the communication with public/private sources for obtaining the data. She will spearhead research projects under the research themes of productivity estimation, GVC, trade/labor, and semiconductor industry. She has been PI for several research grants, including an MOE Tier 2 grant. She is full-time at SMU and will devote 35% effort to the project.

Co-PI: Lin MA

MA Linis Assistant Professor of Economics at SMU. His main research areas include trade and economic geography. Lin is very experienced in quantitative analyses of large-scale spatial models and global sourcing. He will supervise the data compilation / maintenance activities, oversee the projects under the research themes of innovation, and lead the project related to transportation infrastructure. He is full-time at SMU and will devote 25% effort to the project.
