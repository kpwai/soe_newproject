About Us

The Trade Centre was founded by Associate Professor of Economics Chang Pao-Li to tackle the growing complexities of Singapore’s role in global trade. Drawing on deep expertise in firm-to-firm linkages and economic networks, the Centre leads research into how multinational enterprises adapt amid rising trade tensions and shifting production patterns. By mapping these intricate networks and analyzing how global shocks ripple through Singapore’s trade ecosystem, the Centre provides critical insights that support economic resilience, inform policy decisions, and promote sustainable growth in an interconnected world.
